# 01. Business

# Overview

AlSat is a marketplace platform where users can create listings and sell products directly to other users.

Unlike a traditional e-commerce platform, AlSat does not own inventory. Every listing belongs to a seller, and the platform acts as a marketplace between buyers and sellers.

The architecture is designed to start as a simple MVP while supporting future growth into a production-grade platform.

---

# Vision

Build a scalable, secure, and maintainable marketplace platform capable of serving thousands of concurrent users.

The long-term goal is to evolve AlSat into one of Azerbaijan's largest online marketplace platforms.

---

# Business Goals

- Allow users to create listings easily.
- Enable buyers to discover listings quickly.
- Provide secure communication between buyers and sellers.
- Support premium features such as VIP listings.
- Support secure online payments.
- Build a platform that can scale horizontally.

---

# Target Users

### Sellers

Users who want to sell products.

Examples:

- Phones
- Laptops
- Cars
- Apartments
- Furniture
- Electronics

---

### Buyers

Users searching for products.

They can:

- Search listings
- Filter results
- Contact sellers
- Save favorites

---

# Revenue Model

The platform itself does not sell products.

Revenue comes from premium services.

Examples:

- VIP Listings
- Featured Listings
- Homepage Promotion
- Subscription Packages
- Advertisement
- Future Commission-based Sales

---

# MVP Scope

The first version focuses only on core marketplace functionality.

Included:

- User registration
- Login
- Create listing
- Upload images
- Browse listings
- Search
- Categories
- Contact seller

Excluded:

- Chat
- Delivery
- Reviews
- Seller rating
- Online payment
- AI recommendations

These features will be added incrementally.

---

# Long-Term Vision

The project is designed using Domain-Driven Design and Microservices Architecture.

Future features can be added without major architectural changes.

Examples:

- Payment Service
- Promotion Service
- Notification Service
- Recommendation Engine
- Analytics
- AI Search
