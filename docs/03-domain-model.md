# 03. Domain Model

The platform is organized around business domains instead of technical layers.

---

# Core Domains

Identity

Responsible for:

- Users
- Authentication
- Authorization

---

Listing

Responsible for:

- Listings
- Price
- Description
- Status
- Seller
- Location

---

Category

Responsible for:

- Category hierarchy

Examples:

Electronics

Phones

Computers

Vehicles

Furniture

---

Media

Responsible for:

- Images
- Videos
- Future Documents

---

Search

Responsible for:

- Searching
- Filtering
- Sorting

---

Promotion

Responsible for:

- VIP Listings
- Featured Listings
- Homepage Ads

---

Payment

Responsible for:

- Online payments
- Payment history
- Premium purchases

---

Notification

Responsible for:

- Email
- SMS
- Push notifications

---
